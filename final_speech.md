# 🎤 FINAL SPEECH — "What if AI Suddenly Disappeared?"
# Level: A2–B1 | Time: ~3 min 30 sec

- ⏸⏸ = длинная пауза (2 сек), дай аудитории осмыслить
- 😄 = лёгкий тон, можно улыбнуться
- 🔊 = говори чуть громче / с акцентом

## **[СЛАЙД 1]** INTRO — ~25 сек

Hello, everyone.

I study Software Engineering. ⏸
And today I want to talk about something that concerns every single one of us. ⏸⏸

🔊 **What if AI suddenly disappeared?**

*[пауза, оглядись на аудиторию]*

Not "what if it got worse". Not "what if it changed". ⏸
Just — gone. Tomorrow morning. Forever. ⏸⏸

😄 I thought about this question for a while...
and honestly, it scared me a little.

---

## **[СЛАЙД 2]** THE SCENARIO — ~30 сек

*[этот слайд — атмосфера, не зачитывай иконки]*

Imagine you wake up. ⏸
You open your laptop. ⏸
You go to ChatGPT... and the page doesn't load. ⏸⏸

You try GitHub Copilot. ⏸ Nothing.
Google Assistant. ⏸ Nothing.

Just a blank screen. ⏸ And silence. ⏸⏸

😄 For most students — that IS a horror movie.

---

## **[СЛАЙД 3]** GRAMMAR — ~20 сек

*[этот слайд — быстро, просто ориентир для аудитории]*

In my presentation I use two grammar structures. ⏸

**First Conditional** — for things that are real and possible. ⏸
**Second Conditional** — for things that are imaginary and hypothetical. ⏸

Let's start with the real ones.

---

## **[СЛАЙД 4]** STUDENTS — ~45 сек

So. If AI disappears tomorrow — what happens to students? ⏸⏸

Well. ⏸ We write our own essays. ⏸
By ourselves. ⏸ With our own words. ⏸⏸

😄 I know, I know — shocking.

But think about it seriously for a second. ⏸
Right now, a lot of students don't really write — they edit.
They give AI a topic, get a text, change a few words... and submit. ⏸⏸

If that's gone — ⏸ we actually have to think. ⏸
We have to find information ourselves. ⏸
We have to structure our ideas. ⏸⏸

And honestly? ⏸ That will take ten times longer. ⏸
😄 Goodbye, free time. Hello, library at midnight.

---

## **[СЛАЙД 5]** IT WORLD — ~40 сек

Now — the IT world. ⏸ My future workplace. 😄 ⏸⏸

Right now, AI writes code, suggests fixes, explains errors...
It's basically a very smart colleague who never sleeps. ⏸⏸

If that colleague disappears — ⏸
developers will make more mistakes. ⏸
Bugs will come back in huge numbers. ⏸
Teams will be slower. ⏸⏸

And the funniest part? ⏸
A lot of junior developers have never actually debugged without AI help. ⏸
😄 If Copilot disappears — some of them will be completely lost.

Including, possibly, me. ⏸⏸

But hey — at least we'll finally learn syntax properly. 😄

---

## **[СЛАЙД 6]** HYPOTHETICAL — ~50 сек

*[тон меняется — чуть задумчивее, философски]*

Now let's go deeper. ⏸⏸

What if AI had **never** existed at all? ⏸⏸

First — school students. ⏸
Think about kids today. ⏸
Many of them google everything, ask AI everything. ⏸
Calculator for maths. AI for essays. Google for spelling. ⏸⏸

If AI didn't exist — ⏸ they would actually have to think. ⏸
Remember things. Solve problems with their own heads. ⏸⏸

😄 I know that sounds old-fashioned. But maybe it's not such a bad thing.

Second — ⏸ the IT field. ⏸⏸

We have a word in Russian — "вкатун". ⏸
It means someone who enters IT just because it pays well — ⏸
without real interest, without real skills. ⏸⏸

With AI, it's very easy to pretend. ⏸
You ask ChatGPT to write a program, ⏸
copy the code, ⏸
and call yourself a developer. ⏸⏸

😄 If AI didn't exist — ⏸ those people would probably choose a different career.
Very quickly. ⏸⏸

And personally? ⏸
If I didn't have AI — ⏸
I would study twice as hard, sleep half as much, and drink three times more coffee. ⏸⏸

😄 Would I be a better programmer? ⏸ Probably yes.
Would I survive the first year? ⏸⏸ ...Hard to say.

---

## **[СЛАЙД 7]** SILVER LINING — ~35 сек

But here's the thing — ⏸ not everything would be worse. ⏸⏸

If AI disappeared, people would have to be more creative. ⏸
Real, original ideas would matter again. ⏸
Because you can't just generate them. ⏸⏸

Artists would have more work. ⏸
Teachers would actually have to teach. ⏸
And students — ⏸ would actually have to learn. ⏸⏸

😄 I'm not saying it would be easy.
But maybe it would be more... honest.

---

## **[СЛАЙД 8]** CONCLUSION — ~25 сек

So — my conclusion is this. ⏸⏸

A world without AI would be harder. ⏸ Slower. ⏸ More exhausting. ⏸⏸

But it would also force us to be smarter, more creative, and more real. ⏸⏸

🔊 And personally — ⏸⏸

If AI disappeared tomorrow... ⏸
I would probably cry for a week. ⏸⏸

😄 And then I would open a textbook.

For the first time in a long time.

⏸⏸

**Thank you. Any questions?** ⏸ 😄 AI is not allowed to answer them.
